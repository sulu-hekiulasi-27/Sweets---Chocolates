About Us

Sweets & Chocolates Galore started in 1997 when we saw a need for more exotic options in the sweets and chocolates area. We monitored the overseas market and found there were certain favorites that were in demand in various countries.

We set up a small shop in the Atrium mall and started importing specialized ranges of sweets and chocolates from Italy, France, and some of the Asian markets where they have very different varieties.

Since we opened our website, word has got around and now the majority of our sales are online. Please email an order to us and we will endeavor to fulfill your needs. Thanks for your continuing support.

Helen
Managing Director
